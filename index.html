About Us

At Ais, we view cleaning not merely as a surface-level task, but as the way living spaces breathe. By blending chemistry and technology with a meticulous focus on human health, we redefine 'deep purity' standards in every product—from powder detergents to personal care. In our specialized field of powder detergent technology, we offer molecular solutions that eliminate the toughest stains without damaging fibers. Ais is more than just a cleaning brand; it is the scientific embodiment of the flawless freshness you feel in your home and on your skin.

Trusted Partner

Sustainability

Environmentally friendly solutions that protect both your spaces and our planet.

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform traditional cleaning concepts through modern laboratory techniques and eco-friendly formulas. We believe that cleanliness should be an accessible 'living standard' for everyone, not just a necessity. Across every product group—from our flagship powder detergents to wet wipes—we combine high performance with reliability, striving to represent the purest form of hygiene on a global scale.

Our Vision

As a pioneer of innovation in hygiene technologies, our vision is to become a global reference point that defines the very criteria of 'purity.' By elevating cleaning culture to a more sustainable, smarter, and more effective level with every formula we develop, we aim to establish a permanent guarantee of absolute hygiene wherever the Ais logo is present.

Innovation

Continuously developing new technologies and formulas to meet evolving cleaning needs.

Global Reach

Expanding our presence in international markets with sustainable and effective solutions.
